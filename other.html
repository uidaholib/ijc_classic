Smaller Collections

Jane Jarvis Collection

Jane Jarvis was a jazz pianist. The collection includes newspaper clippings, concert fliers and programs, arrangements, scores, and a Thomas electronic organ.

Gerry Mulligan Collection

Gerry Mulligan was a jazz saxophonist, composer, arranger, and bandleader. This collection consists mostly of digitized scores and other materials of the Gerry Mulligan Library. The Library of Congress holds the originals, which span the years circa 1940-1994. The materials were digitized in 2003.

Yuri Vermenich Collection

Vermenich is a Russian jazz historian. The collection consists of fifteen signed photographs and nine letters from American jazz musicians, and seven books about jazz that he translated to Russian.

Jazz Collection

Memorabilia and other Jazz-related materials held by Special Collections & Archives .
